## How to Sign the CLA

You only need to sign this CLA if Enable Security has explicitly asked you to do so for a proposed contribution.

### Signing Process

1. **Read this CLA carefully** and ensure you understand and agree to all terms.

2. **Send an email to cla@enablesecurity.com** with the following:
   - **Subject:** "DVRTC CLA Signature"
   - **Body:** "I have read and agree to the DVRTC Contributor License Agreement"
   - **Include:**
     - Your full legal name
     - Your GitHub username or account handle
     - Your email address
     - Optional: Company name (if contributing on behalf of an organization)

3. **Wait for confirmation:** Enable Security will confirm separately if the contribution process should continue.

### Corporate Contributors

If you are contributing on behalf of your employer or company, your employer may need to sign a Corporate CLA. Please contact cla@enablesecurity.com to arrange this.
